About

The Agency Calculator is a tool designed to help measure and better understand the level of personal agency experienced by women in different areas of life. It evaluates key factors such as autonomy, decision-making power, access to opportunities, and the ability to influence personal and social outcomes. By translating these complex concepts into clear, measurable insights, the calculator helps highlight both strengths and areas where greater empowerment may be needed. The goal is to provide a simple yet meaningful way to better understand women’s lived experiences and support conversations, research, and initiatives that promote independence, confidence, and greater agency for women.
